Plant Care Guide

Keeping track of watering schedules is the hardest part of owning plants. Use this table as a quick reference.

Plant Name Light Level Watering Frequency
Snake Plant Low to Bright Every 2-3 weeks
Monstera Deliciosa Bright Indirect Weekly (when topsoil dry)
Pothos Low to Medium Weekly
Succulents Direct Sun Monthly (soak thoroughly)
Fiddle Leaf Fig Bright Indirect Weekly (consistent moisture)
