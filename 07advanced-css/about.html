Routine: Watering Day Checklist

  1. Check the soil moisture with a finger test.
  2. Take plants to the sink for a thorough soak.
  3. Let them drain completely before returning to the shelf.

Plant Care Glossary

Variegation
The appearance of differently colored zones in the leaves, and sometimes the stems, of plants.
Root Bound
When a plant's roots have filled the pot and have no room to grow, often circling the bottom.
Drainage
The ability of water to flow out of the soil so the roots do not rot.
